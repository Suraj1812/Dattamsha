# Dattamsha HR Policies

## Leave Policy
Employees are eligible for 24 paid leave days per calendar year, excluding public holidays.
Managers must approve planned leave at least 3 business days in advance unless emergency leave applies.

## Flexible Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Teams handling customer-critical operations must ensure shift coverage during core hours.

## Performance and Development
Formal performance check-ins occur quarterly.
Managers are expected to schedule at least one monthly growth conversation with each direct report.

## Wellbeing Policy
If overtime exceeds 25 hours in a month for two consecutive months, managers must initiate a workload review.
Employees can request confidential wellbeing support through HR at any time.

## Code of Conduct
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are strictly prohibited.
All concerns may be escalated to HR or Ethics without fear of retaliation.
