# Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4)
- Build canonical workforce model and ingestion connectors.
- Establish data quality checks and lineage tags.
- Stand up baseline org health dashboards.

## Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8)
- Train and calibrate attrition and burnout models.
- Add cohort segmentation and trend/anomaly detection.
- Ship manager-level risk and intervention views.

## Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12)
- Add nudge policy engine with channel adapters (Slack/Teams/Email).
- Implement nudge feedback capture and recommendation ranking.
- Introduce simulation workflows for executive what-if decisions.

## Phase 4 (Weeks 13-16)
- Deploy policy assistant with RAG and policy lifecycle ingestion.
- Add controls for DPDP/GDPR workflows, audits, and retention.
- Harden multi-tenant design and enterprise SSO.
